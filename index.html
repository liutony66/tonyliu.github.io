I am a Bioengineering PhD student at Stanford, interested in developing better treatments for psychiatric conditions, such as major depression and addiction. I previously received a BS in Physics at Yale, and have worked on systems neuroscience in small brains and closed-loop neuromodulation for treatment-resistant depression. I'm grateful to be funded by the Knight-Hennessy Fellowship.
